About

Written or spoken, whether written or spit out, It doesn't seem to play a role in the mechanism of my thinking. The fundamental element of my mental thinking is a certain sign Which can be reproduced or combined (combined)'as desired', It is a somewhat clear image.
Albert Einstein[1]

During this pandemic, we have confirmed that the distinction between online and the real world is meaningless and that it already coexisted in the real world. And we were able to reaffirm the smartness of mankind to quickly adapt and cope with reality. In order not to deviate from such smart, companies produce, intellectuals are solidarity, and artists exhibit as usual. We praise transhumanism with the advancement of science and technology, and the progress of mankind is moving forward without looking back. Because smart is only for development and profit generation, and other than that, it is a loser.
Increasingly, the logic of capital is becoming clearer and people believe that there is no other alternative. However, the ideal smart society without alternatives is still homeless, full of people who are hungry, exploited, and untreated in hospitals, and the state hires people to legally war. Does the development of science and technology really correlate with the development of civilization?

The system of the New Order is a world completely digitally controlled. This is no different from slavery. Capitalists have persuaded the public to dispose of old and familiar things for the survival of mankind through this pandemic, and are immersing themselves in digital archives and starting commercialization of prepared robots and artificial intelligence. As an alternative, capitalists are designing a ontrolled society that can turn off noise and monitor conflicts with data amid the overproduction of neoliberalism. We are entering a controlled society, nodding to become the stormtroopers of capitalists, to represent them, to be smart, not to be losers.

To not be a stormtrooper for capitalists We have to pause and think about Humanization.
